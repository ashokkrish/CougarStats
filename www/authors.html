Etymology

The name CougarStats is inspired by Mount Royal University's athletics mascot, the cougar, symbolizing strength and agility, and by the app's focus on statistics.

An iOS application with similar functionality, maintained separately from this R Shiny web application, is available from the Apple App Store under the name CougarStats. Ashok Krishnamurthy provides statistical supervision and design requirements for the application.
